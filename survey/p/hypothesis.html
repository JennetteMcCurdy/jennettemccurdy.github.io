Hypothesis Testing

Hypothesis:

On average, people care about their privacy at a value higher than 3, from 1 to 5.

H₀: M = 3

H₁: M > 3


One Sample Z Test Formula:

t = (x̄ - μ) / (s / √n)

x̄ = 3.29

μ = 3

s = 1.27

n = 51

z = 1.645

Significance: 0.05

Z (Cutoff): 1.645


t = (3.29 - 3) / (1.27 / √51) = 1.63


Not significant evidence to prove more people care about their privacy.
